Furawashi

Project by ViviOn Japan

What we do:

In this project, PADMA Studio collaborated on the process of replacing the background for the webtoon titled Furawashi. The scope of work included designing the angles and perspectives of 3D assets provided by the client. The PADMA Studio team worked to ensure that the backgrounds aligned with the storyline and the perspective of the storyboard images. We handled the line art, base textures, and shadows on different layers as needed for the job.

In the next step, we imported each layer into Photoshop files, which were then arranged according to the story and storyboard.Content updates and copywriting improvements were also part of the project.

Creative Process

PADMA Studio began by analyzing the 3D assets and storyboard provided by the client. Our team focused on designing the appropriate angles and perspectives to match the scenes. Using tools like Adobe Photoshop, we created line art, base textures, and shadows, layering them to achieve the desired depth and detail. Each layer was meticulously crafted to ensure it complemented the webtoon’s aesthetic and storyline.

Creative Process
